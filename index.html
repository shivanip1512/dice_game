  • This is a Two player game.
  • Active player can roll the dice until he gets 1 on dice or he holds his dice score.
  • If Player gets 1 on dice, the current score will get reset to 0 and chance for rolling will be passes on to other Player.
  • If a Player wants to hold his current score, he can click on hold button thus resulting in addition of current Score to Score of Player and the chance will be passed to other player.
  • The one who reaches Score of 100 first will win the game.
